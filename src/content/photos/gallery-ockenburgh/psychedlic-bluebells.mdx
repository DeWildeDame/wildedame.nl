Some silly attempts to get something decent out of an modified helios 44-2. 
The helios 44-2 lenses are famous for their flexibility when it comes to modification and are a staple of modern cinema. The Batman (2022) made heavy use of modified helios 44-2 lenses, although they are popular you can find them for less than 50 euro's.

In the case of these photos the front element of the lens is inverted, this prevents the lens from ever really focussing. Everything will remain soft and fuzzy. In the next iteration I will be narrowing the flange distance. This will effectively make it easier to focus on closeby subjects but forfeits long focus distances.
